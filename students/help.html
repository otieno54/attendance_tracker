Need Help?

Welcome to the Help Center. Here you can find answers to common questions and get in touch with support if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

📌 How do I check my attendance?

You can view your attendance by navigating to the "Attendance" page from the sidebar. It shows daily logs and overall attendance stats.

📌 How do I enroll in a course?

Click on "Enroll" in the sidebar and select the course you want to enroll in, then submit your request.

📌 Who do I contact for technical issues?

You can contact the IT support team via email at support@attendancetracker.com.
