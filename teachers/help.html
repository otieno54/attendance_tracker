Help & Support

If you need assistance, check out our frequently asked questions (FAQs) or contact us directly for help.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I download the attendance report?

To download the attendance report, simply select a date range and click the "Download Report" button. The report will be generated in CSV format.

2. How do I change my settings?

Click on the "Settings" link in the sidebar to adjust your preferences, including theme and notifications.

3. How do I provide feedback?

Navigate to the "Feedback" section in the sidebar and fill out the feedback form. Let us know what you think!
